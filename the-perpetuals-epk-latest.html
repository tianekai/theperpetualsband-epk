Who We Are

Music That Lives Forever

The Perpetuals are a hard-rocking cover band with a deep love for the music that defined rock's golden era — and the chops to do it justice. From Foo Fighters to Foreigner, CCR to AC/DC, we cover the artists that matter.

With three fully-loaded sets spanning classic rock, 90s alternative, blues rock, and modern country-rock, we tailor every show to the crowd — whether it's a late-night club set or a packed festival stage.

We're tight, loud, and genuinely passionate about every song we play. No backing tracks. No shortcuts. Just a great live band doing what they love.

We travel with a professional sound team dedicated to keeping our sound and volume perfect for any venue — from intimate private events to large festival stages. You'll never have to worry about the mix.
